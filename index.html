Recommended regions

    Carbon footprint is approximated using the yearly carbon free energy percentage and average electricity carbon intensity of the region (source). Google invests in enough renewable energy and carbon offsets to neutralize the global operational carbon emissions footprint of Google Cloud per the GHG protocol under the Scope 2 market-based methodology.
    Latency is approxiated using physical distance between selected countries and the city or country of the region.
    Price is approximated using Google Compute Engine E2 instance vCPU price.
    All processing is happening client side in your browser. Data is never uploaded.

    This is not an officially supported Google product. Read more about Google Cloud sustainability efforts.
